RESPONDENTS PRESENT LIVING CONDITIONS

How would you describe your own present living conditions?

Overall, 58 percent of respondents believe their living conditions are fairly or very good while 42 percent feel their conditions are either bad or very bad. Positive sentiment was well above average in the north east, where 80 percent of respondents think their living conditions are good. Living conditions were found to be worse than average for respondents in the central highlands, where 60 percent feel conditions are bad or very bad. The situation also seems worse than average in the west.
