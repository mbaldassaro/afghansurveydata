WHY COUNTRY IS GOING IN WRONG DIRECTION

We asked survey respondents that felt the country was heading the wrong direction "Why do you think the country is heading the wrong direction?" Among those respondents, the reasons they provided for their pessimistic views varied however political instability and insecurity, neighboring countries appeared to be common threads. The presence of the Taliban, official corruption, insecurity, neigbhoring countries, and the poor economy were each offered as reasons for pessimism by more than 10% of respondents.
