WHY COUNTRY IS GOING IN RIGHT DIRECTION

Why do you think the country is heading the right direction?

Among those respondents who felt the country was heading in the right direction, the reasons they provided for their optimistic views varied however political stability and security appeared to be common threads. Although no single reason underpinned the views of a significant proportion of those who believed the country was heading in the right direction, the formation of a National Unity Government following the 2014 elections, ongoing provision of international assistance, good security, and existence of a legitimate constitution were each offered as reasons for optimism by more than 10% of respondents.
