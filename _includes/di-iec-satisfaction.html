SATISFIED WITH IEC & IECC PERFORMANCE IN 2014 ELECTIONS

Are you satisfied with the performance of the IEC during the 2014 elections? Are you satisfied with performance of the IECC during the 2014 elections?

We asked Afghans familiar with the functions of the Independent Election Commission (IEC) and Independent Electoral Complaints Commission (IECC) whether they are satisfied with the institutionsâ€™ performance during the 2014 elections. Forty-eight percent of respondents reported they are familiar with the function of the IEC, and of those, three-in-five respondents are satisfied with its performance. Thirty-two percent of respondents reported they are familiar with the function of the IECC, and of those, three-in-five respondents are satisfied with its performance. There was notably higher than average satisfaction with the institutions in the south and east.
