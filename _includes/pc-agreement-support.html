SUPPORT FOR SEVEN-POINT POLITICAL AGREEMENT

The agreement between Dr. Abdullah and President Ghani regarding the structure of the National Unity Government included several points. How supportive are you of each of the following?

The National Unity Government (NUG) agreement signed by President Ghani and Dr. Abdullah includes provisions for the role of the Chief Executive Officer, the appointment of senior officials, and electoral reform, among other items. Respondents were overwhelmingly positive on all points. Large majorities of respondents were at least somewhat supportive on all points with the strongest support for issuing electronic identification cards for all citizens.
