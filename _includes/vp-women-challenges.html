CHALLENGES TO WOMEN'S PARTICIPATION IN ELECTIONS

What are the two main factors that prevent women from participating in elections?

A majority of both men and women cited insecurity as the primary factor preventing women from voting, however men were more likely to report this. Family members preventing women from voting was the second most common response and was cited by more women than men. Thirty percent of both men and women think a lack of awareness about elections prevents women from voting. A year before, about half of respondents thought that lack of awareness prevented women from participating in elections, illustrating some success in civic education efforts targeting women.
